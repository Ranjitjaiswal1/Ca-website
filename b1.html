Frequently Asked Questions

How do we get started with your services?

You can contact us via phone, email, or by filling out the contact form on our website. We will schedule an initial consultation to understand your needs and provide a proposal.

Is your firm registered with ICAN?

Yes, we are a registered audit firm licensed by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nepal (ICAN), ensuring full compliance with national regulations.

Do you offer consulting for startups and SMEs?

Yes, we provide tailored consulting services for startups and small to medium-sized enterprises to help them grow efficiently.

Are your audits compliant with Nepal Financial Reporting Standards (NFRS)?

Absolutely. All our audits strictly adhere to NFRS and other applicable national standards.

Do you also help with tax filing and returns?

Yes, we assist individuals and businesses with tax planning, filing, and compliance services.

Who needs to get an audit in Nepal?

Businesses registered under the Company Act, NGOs, INGOs, and certain entities above specified thresholds are required to get audited annually as per Nepalese law.
